UI/UX Evaluation of Chelsea FC News Article

Published on November 4, 2025 by Eazi

Website Overview

The Chelsea Football Club Website (https://www.chelseafc.com/en) Is the official digital platform for Chelsea F.C, a Premier League football club based in London. It provides fans with club news, player updates, match information, videos, and exclusive content. This evaluation focuses specifically on the news article titled "Season ticket benefits - take the Stadium Tour for free!" published under the News section.

Page Screenshot

Site Details

  • URL of the website: https://www.chelseafc.com/en/news/article/season-ticket-benefits-take-the-stadium-tour-for-free?
  • Name of the website: Chelsea FC Official Website
  • Target audience:Chelsea fans, football followers, and season ticket holders seeking club updates, promotions, and news.

Site Organization

The website is organized with a top navigation bar seperating content into categories like Teams, News, Matches. The News section uses a card-based grid layout that allows users to browse articlesby topic or date. The article page follows a vertical scroll structure: a feature image, headline, publication details, and the body text. Supporting links and related stories appear below, helping users continue exploring.

CRAP Design Principles

  • Contrast:The site uses strong contrast with white text over navy-blue banners and black text on white background for easy readability.
  • Alignment: Content follows a centered, grid-based layout. The article title, image, and text are cleanly aligned to maintain visual order.

Accessibility Evaluation

The Audit score according to the Accessibility Checker wasn't that bad, the overall score was 71%. Here are the details: Critical issues(18), Passed Audits(37), Reqquired Manuel Audits(3), and Not Applicable(48).

Site's Effectiveness

The article effectively communicates information about season tickets benefits and free stadium tour promotion. Eligibility and dates are easy to locate.

Site's Efficiency

Navigation and layout support quick access to content. The text loads quickly, and minimal distractions keep focus on the main page.

Engagement

The design is modern and visually appealing, consistent with Chelsea's professional brand. High quality image, clear typography, and responsive design create a pleasant reading experience. The visuals fits the sports and fan engagement context, encouraging users to explore related news.

Recommendation

To improve user experience, Chelsea Fc could ensure that every icon and image include accurate alt text which could push accessibility closer to a perfect score. With what have learnt so far and according to the result i got from the accessibility checker. I think they can work on that.
