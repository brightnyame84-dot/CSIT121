JPG Format

About This Image

This photograph shows my teammates and I before a soccer match. We took this picture because the team jersey was new, and we all liked the yellow and blue colors. The photo captures our team spirit and excitement before the game.

Why JPG Format?

JPG format works well for this photograph because it handles detailed colors and gradients efficiently while keeping the file size reasonable for web use. Since this is an original photo, JPG preserves the visual quality of the image without creating a large file.
